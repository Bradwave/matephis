	3. $f(x) = \ln(x^2)$
	4. $f(x) = \ln(x^2 - 1)$
	5. $f(x) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(x)}$
	6. $f(x) = e^{\frac 1  x}$
	7. $f(x) = \sqrt{x^2 - 1}$
	8. $f(x) = \sqrt{x^3 - x}$
	9. $f(x) = (\sin x)^2$ per $x \in (0, 2\pi)$
	10. $f(x) = \sqrt{\sin x}$

	12. $f(x) = (\sin x)^3$ per $x \in (0, 2pi)$

	14. $f(x) = \ln e^x$
	15. $f(x) = \arctan \frac 1 x$
	16. $f(x) = \arctan x^2$
